1. ✅ PostgreSQL has been deployed as a StatefulSet.
2. 🛠️ To connect to your PostgreSQL instance, use the following DNS address:

   my-database-0.my-database.<namespace>.svc.cluster.local:5432

   Replace <namespace> with the namespace where this chart is deployed.

3. 🔐 Credentials:
   - User: postgres
   - Password: mysecretpassword
   - Database: mydb

4. 📦 To connect from a Pod in the same namespace, use:

   psql -h my-database-0.my-database -U postgres -d mydb

5. 🚀 To upgrade or delete this release:

   helm upgrade my-db ./my-database
   helm uninstall my-db
